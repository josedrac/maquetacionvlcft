Tapas, Wine & History Tour – 65,00 €  (includes VAT and all food and drinks)

  • Enjoy an authentic Valencia eating experience: a small group dinner consisting of delicious Spanish food – tapas (traditional small plates and bites) – and local quality wines at 4 traditional bars and restaurants that have been serving quality food for many decades.
  • Cool down with a delectable all natural ice cream made of turron – a local delicacy from Alicante.
  • On this brand new guided walking tapas tour you will spend a fun evening exploring Valencia’s bustling barrio of L’Eixample – the neoclassical district of Valencia, the historic village of Ruzafa and the nightlife hub of Cánovas. Truly, this is Valencia off the beaten track!
  • If you love food and culture, this is a great thing to do in Valencia!

The History of Valencia Through Food

I am on a mission to bring the real Valencia to travelers. On my food tours you will not find franchises, chain restaurants, mediocre places or tourist traps. Join me to learn about Valencia’s legendary establishments that have been open for decades (some – over a century!). Meet the owners who inherited business from their parents and proudly keep local traditions alive to this day. Taste the authentic food made from the best ingredients. Explore the special off-the-beaten-track neighborhoods and eat traditional Valencia food where locals do!

Become a Valencian for a few hours… Join us!
